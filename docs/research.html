What is Our Research Question?

How can the main Final Fantasy VII characters' importance be measured through the number of times the character talks and number of times the character is talked about?

This research question was chosen as it allows us to use our coding knowledge to understand the importance of each of the members of the party. For instance, Cloud may be notably more significant than another party member like Yuffie because he is the protagonist and not an unlockable character.
